DE_Lidl (Bomb/Defuse) 

by Cmdr 

A supermarket is selling cheap imported kebaps.
This harms a near kebap store, which cannot keep up with the cheap price.

For revenge, the kebap store owner hires some terrorists,
to blow up the supermarket with it's cheap kebaps.

Terrorists:
Blow up the cheap kebap at the supermarket!

Counter-Terrorists:
Stop these rebels! Long live capitalism!

Other notes:
There is only one bomb target in this mission.
